TechnoRunner Privacy policy :

Uses features from the Google Play Games API.
As such, some of the information of your Google Play Games account are shared with other users :
- Google Play Games Account Name : will be shown to everyone in leaderboards.
- Google Play Games PlayerID : is used internally by the Leaderboard system, with no external access.

You can opt out of both of those by changing your Google Play Games account parameters.

No other info is collected by the game.
